At Splash, our purpose is to connect people with the raw, exhilarating beauty of nature by facilitating safe and unforgettable adventures on the river.

History

Splash Rafting was founded in 2025 by a college student Evard Mabounda. Tired of large, impersonal outfitters, they started Splash on the Salmon River with a focus on personalized, safe, and environmentally conscious trips. Starting with just two rafts and deep local knowledge, their passion quickly earned them a strong reputation. Over three decades, Splash grew from a small operation in an old barn into a leading outfitter, recognized for maintaining its core commitment.
